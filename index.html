About Me

  • I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Jose Martinez. My research focuses on massive MIMO millimeter-wave radar system, millimeter-wave mesh network and communication, and antenna and ASIC design for communication and sensing applications.
  • I received my Master Degree in Electronics Science and Technology at Zhejiang University in March 2017, where I worked on MIMO-OFDM wireless communication system design with ultra-low receiver complexity, software-defined radio and digital signal processing under the supervision of Prof. Xianmin Zhang. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor Degree in Electronics and Technology at Zhejiang University of Technology in June 2014.
